Things to keep in mind before you Guess The Frame!

  • You can move between the next and previous frame.
  • You can finish the guessing anytime by clicking the "Finish" button.
  • Your answered frames are marked as "✓"
  • You get 2 minutes to guess all frames. Keep an eye on the progress bar!
  • If you don't answer all of them in the given time frame, the guessing will automatically conclude and you will get your result based on finished answers.
  • When you finish the test, you will see your total correct answers in form of a score! Also a "Winner" or "Loser" indicator based on your percentage!
  • Keep in mind that if you leave the browser window or change the browser tab, the test will conclude automatically!
  • The test will start as soon as you click the "Lemme Guess" button below. Take your time and let the guessing begin!
